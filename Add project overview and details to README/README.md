#AR for Rural Education

#Bridging the Gap through UX & Research

#Overview

This project explores how **Augmented Reality (AR)** combined with **User Experience (UX) design** can transform education in rural India.

It focuses on designing **accessible, low-cost, and user-friendly learning solutions** for schools that face limited infrastructure, low digital literacy, and minimal exposure to interactive tools.

The goal is to move beyond innovation and ensure **real-world feasibility, inclusivity, and impact**.

# Problem Statement

Rural education systems face multiple challenges:

* Limited digital infrastructure
* Lack of engaging and interactive learning tools
* Low student engagement
* Minimal teacher training in technology

Most existing AR solutions:

* Require high-end devices
* Depend on strong internet connectivity
* Have complex interfaces

This makes them **unsuitable for rural environments**.

#Objectives

* Analyze digital literacy levels in rural schools
* Evaluate the effectiveness of AR in subjects like Science and Geography
* Design a **user-friendly AR UX framework**
* Develop **offline-first and low-bandwidth solutions**
* Validate designs through usability testing

#Proposed Solution

A **research-driven UX framework** for AR-based learning that is:

*Simple & intuitive* for first-time users
*Offline-first & low-bandwidth compatible*
*Supported by *teacher training modules*
* 🎮 Designed for *interactive and engaging learning*
*Tailored specifically for **rural constraints**

# Methodology

This project follows a **Human-Centered Design Process**:

1. **Empathize** – Understand user needs and challenges
2. **Define** – Identify key problem areas
3. **Ideate** – Explore AR-based solutions
4. **Prototype** – Build low & high-fidelity designs
5. **Test** – Validate and refine with users
