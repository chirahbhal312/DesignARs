# 📊 Category 3: Pedagogical Impact & Learning Outcomes

## 📌 Project Title
AR for Rural Education: Bridging the Gap through UX and Research

---

## 📖 Overview
This repository focuses on evaluating the **pedagogical impact of Augmented Reality (AR)** in rural education, particularly in STEM learning environments with limited infrastructure.

The goal is to analyze how AR influences:
- Conceptual understanding
- Student engagement
- Learning outcomes
- Language accessibility (vernacular vs English)

---

## 🎯 Research Objectives
- Analyze how AR reduces the abstraction gap in STEM subjects
- Measure improvement in student learning outcomes using AR
- Evaluate engagement levels in AR-based learning
- Compare effectiveness of vernacular vs English AR content

---

## ❓ Research Questions
1. Does AR-based 3D visualization reduce the abstraction gap in STEM subjects?
2. How does language (vernacular vs English) affect learning outcomes in AR environments?
3. Does AR improve student engagement and motivation in rural classrooms?

---

## 🧪 Methodology
- Field Observations in rural schools  
- Semi-structured Interviews (students & teachers)  
- Surveys (pre-test & post-test)  
- Usability Testing of AR prototypes  
- Comparative Analysis (Traditional vs AR learning)

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- Test Scores (Pre vs Post)
- Conceptual Understanding
- Engagement Levels
- Retention Rate
- Language Comprehension

---

## 🧠 Key Concepts
- Abstraction Gap
- Experiential Learning
- Constructivist Learning Theory
- Human-Centered UX Design
